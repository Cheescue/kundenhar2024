ACHIEVEMENTS:

ECO Harmony has emerged as a trailblazer in environmental preservation, championing innovative solutions for a sustainable future. Over the past decade, the organization has spearheaded numerous impactful initiatives, including reforesting over 10,000 hectares of degraded land and establishing 50 eco-friendly community parks worldwide. Through their "Zero Waste Cities" campaign, ECO Harmony has successfully collaborated with municipalities to reduce landfill contributions by 75% in participating regions. Their groundbreaking marine conservation efforts have restored vital coral reefs across 200 kilometers of coastline, boosting marine biodiversity. With educational programs reaching over 500,000 young minds, ECO Harmony continues to inspire a new generation of environmental stewards dedicated to protecting our planet.
